Hope Events

Bringing compassion and necessities to those who need it most.

Our Mission

Snowdrop United is dedicated to making a tangible difference in the lives of those experiencing homelessness. Through community support and direct action, we provide meals, warm clothing, and essential supplies to those in need across the Houston area.


What We Do

  • Food Drives: Collecting and distributing non-perishable food items.
  • Hot Meals: Preparing and serving hot meals in downtown areas.
  • Clothing Distributions: Providing warm coats, blankets, and socks during colder months.
  • Community Connection: Offering a listening ear and a sense of dignity to our neighbors.
